# The Shelf

Items explicitly paused from active work. Single place to look for "what's shelved?"

**Shelved ≠ aspirational.** Shelved = explicitly paused with a why and an unshelve trigger. Aspirational = planned but not started.

## How to shelve something

Add an entry with five fields: Shelved date / Why / Detail pointer / Unshelve trigger / Cost of staying shelved. When something leaves the shelf, move it to "Recently unshelved" or remove it entirely with a dated comment.
